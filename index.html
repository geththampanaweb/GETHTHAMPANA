About ගෙත්තම්පාණ

ගෙත්තම්පාණ is a musical programme organized by D.S. Senanayake College, first introduced in 2025 to promote musical appreciation and provide students with a platform to showcase their talents. Following the success of its inaugural edition, the programme will be held for the second time in 2026 with the theme of Sri Lankan Folk Music.

This year's event aims to celebrate the richness and diversity of Sri Lanka's folk music traditions through performances and presentations that highlight their cultural and historical significance. By bringing traditional music to a modern audience, Geththampana 2026 seeks to foster cultural awareness, encourage artistic expression, and inspire a greater appreciation for the nation's musical heritage among students and the wider community.

Experience live music performances, vibrant stage productions and a night filled with rhythm and emotion.
